The cost of goods is determined for each order individually and in general, is calculated based on the requested quantity and the desired delivery time. Delivery time for specific items may vary depending on the time of the order and the location of the components. The exact delivery dates are indicated in the commercial offer for each specific customer.

To place an order, you must send a request by Quote request form, after which the order will be transferred for processing. After receiving the order in a short time the manager will contact you to discuss the necessary details.
